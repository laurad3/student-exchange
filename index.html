Stuttgart

I arrived to Stuttgart in September as part of my exchange program. I've always wanted to visit Germany and finally got the opportunity to do so. Also I got to focus my studies in Game Design and Visual Media at Hochschule der Medien. The semester is unfortunately coming to an end but I have really enjoyed my stay here. I met lots of other students, learned a new language, even experienced living on my own for the first time.

Fun Fact Schlossplatz is the largest square in Stuttgart

Heidelberg

During our first week in Stuttgart we took a trip to Heidelberg. We visited the old town and went up to the Heidelberg castle. The old town was very beautiful, having most of its buildings still preserved after the second world war.

Fun Fact Heidelberg is the home to the world’s biggest wine barrel

Mercedes Benz Arena

Our next trip was organized to the Mercedes Benz Museum. I've never been very interested in cars, but I did enjoy seeing the classic cars on display. The museum itself was huge having 9 floors and over 160 vehicles.

Fun Fact The Mercedes Benz Museum in Stuttgart holds the
M-Class SUV from Jurassic Park: The Lost World, with the other
two in display at Universal Studios in Orlando, Florida

Weihnachtsmarkt

One of my favorite experiences in Stuttgart was Christmas. Many evenings I'd find myself at the Weihnachtsmarkt, drinking glühwein with friends and enjoying the Gebrannte mandeln. The markets brought people together and created a nice atmosphere.

Fun Fact According to folklore, on the 5th of December (St. Nicholas eve) St.Nicholas
is accompanied by Krampus, a hairy demon who threatens to kidnap
naughty children and take them into his lair. Happy holidays
